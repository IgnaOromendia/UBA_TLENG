### Ejercicio 4

Sean $\alpha = abb$ y $\beta = acb$

- $\alpha\beta = abbacb$
- $(\alpha\beta)^r = bcabba$
- $\beta^r = bca$
- $\beta^r\alpha^r = bcabba$
- $\lambda\alpha = \alpha$
- $\lambda\beta = \beta$
- $\alpha\lambda\beta = \alpha\beta$
- $\alpha^2\lambda^3\beta^2 = \alpha^2\beta^2 = abbabbacbacb$
